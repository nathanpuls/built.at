About built.at

built.at is a platform that lets domain owners publish verified messages using DNS TXT records. By adding a simple TXT record to your domain, you can share messages that are publicly verifiable and uniquely tied to your domain name.

Our goal is to provide a lightweight, trustable way for domains to communicate short messages without needing a hosting service or database. Anyone can view these messages by visiting built.at/[your-domain].

To publish a verified message from your domain, go to your DNS settings, add a TXT record with @ as the host, and enter built.at=YOUR MESSAGE HERE as the value.
